ITAC Public Website (Frontend Development)

For this project, I worked on the public-facing website for the Industrial Training and Assessment Centers (ITAC), part of the Center for Advanced Energy Systems (CAES) at Rutgers University.

I built several pages using Flutter Web, focusing on responsive layouts, accessibility, and a clean design that worked seamlessly across desktop and mobile. I created modular components and reusable UI elements to keep the design consistent with the organization’s branding.

I also collaborated with the full-stack development team to connect the frontend with APIs and backend services, ensuring smooth data integration throughout the site.
